Enchanted Weapons: Blades that can cut through anything, bows that never miss, hammers that return when thrown.
Scrying Orbs: Crystal balls that allow users to see distant places or people.
Rings of Power: Jewelry granting abilities like invisibility, elemental control, or mind reading.
Potions: Brewed concoctions that heal wounds, grant temporary abilities, or transform the drinker.
Spell Scrolls: One-use magic that anyone can activate, containing spells beyond the user's normal abilities.
Soul Gems: Crystals that can trap souls or store magical energy for later use.

Griffins: Majestic creatures with the body of a lion and head and wings of an eagle. Noble and territorial.
Unicorns: Horse-like creatures with a single horn. Symbol of purity, can purify water and heal wounds.
Trolls: Large, regenerative creatures that often live under bridges or in caves. Some turn to stone in sunlight.
Phoenixes: Immortal birds of flame that are reborn from their own ashes when they die.
Krakens: Enormous sea monsters capable of pulling entire ships underwater.
Wyverns: Dragon-like winged creatures with two legs and a barbed tail, often confused with true dragons.

Enchanted Forests: Ancient woodlands filled with magical creatures, fairies, and sometimes darker entities. Trees might be sentient.
Forgotten Ruins: Abandoned cities or temples containing ancient knowledge, traps, and treasures.
Mountain Citadels: Fortresses built into mountainsides, often home to dwarves or secluded wizards.
Desert Oases: Magical pools that sustain life in barren lands, sometimes gateways to other realms.
Underwater Kingdoms: Vast civilizations beneath the waves, home to merfolk and other aquatic species.
Shadow Realms: Parallel dimensions of darkness where normal rules don't apply. Home to demons and nightmares.

Elemental Magic: Control over fire, water, earth, air, and sometimes light and shadow. Most common and accessible form of magic.
Divine Magic: Granted by gods or spiritual entities. Requires faith and devotion. Often used for healing and protection.
Arcane Magic: Academic magic requiring study and understanding of complex formulas. Uses components and often requires spellbooks.
Blood Magic: Powerful forbidden magic using life force. Often considered taboo due to its dark nature.
Wild Magic: Unpredictable, nature-based magic. Sometimes manifests spontaneously in certain regions or bloodlines.
